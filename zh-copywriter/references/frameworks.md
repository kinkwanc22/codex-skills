# Copywriting Frameworks

Use these as starting structures, not rigid templates.

## Social Post Structures

Pain-to-method:
1. Name a concrete pain
2. Explain why common advice fails
3. Give 3-5 practical moves
4. Add a real example
5. Close with a save/share/comment CTA

Contrarian:
1. "Most people think X"
2. "But the real issue is Y"
3. Explain the mechanism
4. Give a better rule
5. Show a quick example

Story-to-insight:
1. Scene
2. Tension
3. Turning point
4. Lesson
5. Reader application

## Title Formulas

- Result + audience: "给新手运营的 7 个提效动作"
- Pain + correction: "你不是不会写文案，是开头太像说明书"
- Curiosity + boundary: "这 3 类标题容易火，但我只建议用第 2 类"
- Before/after: "把一篇普通种草文改成高转化文案，我只动了 4 处"
- Mistake list: "90% 的产品文案，都输在这 5 个细节"

Avoid titles that promise impossible certainty, shame the reader, or rely only on punctuation and buzzwords.

## Editing Checklist

- Opening answers "why should I keep reading?"
- Each paragraph advances one job
- Benefits are tied to reader scenarios
- Claims have proof or are softened
- CTA is specific and low-friction
- Tone matches platform and account persona
- No translationese such as "此外/综上所述" unless formal tone is intended

## Tone Controls

Expert: precise, structured, fewer adjectives.
Founder: direct, observational, decision-oriented.
Teacher: patient, step-by-step, examples first.
Premium: restrained, concrete, no loud urgency.
KOL/social: vivid, opinionated, fast rhythm.
Warm: empathetic, less imperative, more "你可以".
