# Originality And Rewrite Checks

Use this reference when the user provides source text and asks for 改写, 洗稿, 降重, 仿写, or rewritten posts.

## Safe Rewrite Standard

The output should be independently expressible:
- New angle or thesis framing
- New structure and paragraph order
- New examples, analogies, or scenarios where possible
- New transitions and rhythm
- Distinct opening and ending
- No unnecessary preservation of distinctive phrases

Facts, product details, and public information can remain, but the expression should change substantially.

## Practical Process

1. Extract factual claims and core ideas into neutral bullet points.
2. Decide the new reader promise and platform.
3. Create a fresh outline.
4. Draft from the outline, not from the source sentence by sentence.
5. Compare against the source for overly similar order, imagery, phrasing, or title.

## Refuse Or Redirect

Do not help with:
- Evading plagiarism or copyright detection
- Rewriting a specific living creator's distinctive style on request
- Producing near-duplicates meant to impersonate another author or account
- Removing attribution from quoted or licensed text

Offer alternatives:
- "我可以帮你做原创化改写，保留事实但换角度、结构和表达。"
- "我可以提炼这篇的观点，再写一版适合你账号人设的新稿。"
- "我可以做竞品文案分析，然后产出不复刻表达的新方案。"

## Similarity Red Flags

Revise again if:
- Paragraph order mirrors the source
- More than one distinctive phrase remains
- The title only changed a few words
- Examples and metaphors are the same
- The ending repeats the source's call to action
