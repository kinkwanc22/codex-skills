## Original Source Opening Score

When scoring unexpanded source scripts, add a separate 20-point opening check. This does not replace the 100-point rubric; it helps decide which source is most likely to become a viral expanded long-form draft.

High-performing opening archetypes:

- Dark reveal: 今天聊个黑暗的、很脏、禁术、无解、毫无下限.
- Result promise: 如果你能听懂、如果你掌握、降维打击、彻底重塑、推进关系.
- Identity-shame naming: 舔狗、beta、供养者、没框架、工具人、被女人定义.
- Numbered collection: 八大、十六个、十八条、三十六个、总偏集、所有类型、点赞收藏、随时查阅.
- Counterintuitive mechanism: 为什么、答案只有一个、其实很简单、底层逻辑、普通人以为...其实...
- Crisis defense: 危险信号、废物测试、挖墙脚、被替代、撤离、重新思考相处策略.

20-point opening score:

| Dimension | Points | Standard |
|---|---:|---|
| First-30-character hook | 5 | Strong question, taboo word, result promise, or direct pain point. |
| Mechanism / reversal | 5 | First 100 characters include a mechanism name or counterintuitive judgment. |

- 15-20: strong expansion priority.

- Below 10: lower priority unless likes are unusually high or the topic can be rebuilt.
